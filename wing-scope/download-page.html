WING Scope

A small no-install utility for collecting what a Behringer WING reports about connected AES50, StageConnect, Card, and Module devices.

How to send a report

  1. Open WING Scope.
  2. Type what AES50 or StageConnect gear is connected to the WING.
  3. Enter the WING IP address and click Scan System.
  4. Save the report file.
  5. Email the saved report to crrecording@gmail.com.

WING Scope is a temporary field-data utility for WingMan I/O development.
