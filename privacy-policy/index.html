WingMan I/O Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026
Product: WingMan I/O
Website: https://wingmanio.com/
Seller: BrickHouse Productions LLC
Contact: support@wingmanio.com

This Privacy Policy explains how BrickHouse Productions LLC collects, uses, stores, and protects information related to WingMan I/O, including the website, desktop software, licensing, purchases, updates, and support.

By using WingMan I/O, visiting the website, purchasing a license, starting a trial, activating the software, or contacting support, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

WingMan I/O is a desktop software application for Windows and macOS.

WingMan I/O is not a cloud-hosted application. The app does not automatically collect analytics, telemetry, or crash reports.

The app may connect to the internet for license activation and user-initiated update checks.

2. Information We May Collect

Customer and Purchase Information

When you purchase WingMan I/O, BrickHouse Productions LLC may receive or store information related to your purchase, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Product purchased
  • Order number
  • Purchase date
  • License key
  • License status
  • Activation status
  • Refund status
  • Chargeback status
  • Country or region associated with the purchase

Purchases and licensing are expected to be processed through Lemon Squeezy.

Payment information, including credit card processing, is handled by Lemon Squeezy or related payment providers. BrickHouse Productions LLC does not directly store full credit card numbers.

License Activation Information

WingMan I/O may collect or process information needed to activate and validate licenses, including:

  • License key
  • Machine ID or device identifier
  • Activation ID
  • App version
  • Operating system
  • Activation date
  • Deactivation date
  • Trial status
  • License validation status
  • Number of activated computers

This information is used to manage the two-computer activation limit, allow deactivation and transfer, prevent unauthorized use, provide support, and verify license status.

Trial Information

WingMan I/O offers a 7-day trial. The trial may use local or license-related information to determine trial status, expiration, and activation availability.

Update Check Information

WingMan I/O may check for updates only when the user clicks a check-for-updates option.

When you manually check for updates, the app may send or receive limited technical information such as:

  • Current app version
  • Operating system
  • Update eligibility
  • License or channel status, if required

Support Communications

If you contact support, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Message contents
  • Screenshots or files you choose to send
  • Diagnostic details you choose to provide
  • License key or order information needed to help you
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide

Support is handled by email at: support@wingmanio.com.

Local App and Device Information

WingMan I/O may discover, display, use, or save information related to your local audio system and network, including:

  • Console IP addresses
  • Device names
  • Routing labels
  • Input and output names
  • Device profile information
  • AES50, StageConnect, USB, expansion card, and Dante-related routing information
  • Project files
  • Device Map/topology data
  • Connection status
  • Local configuration settings

This information is used to provide the app’s routing, patching, device mapping, and I/O management features.

Local project files and configuration files may remain on your computer unless you delete them.

WingMan I/O is not intended to collect, upload, or transmit your live audio, recorded audio, full network traffic, or private show content.
